I'm Kirill Ioannidi, father of a girl, and also an entrepreneur in my soul just like you. I understand your difficulties and your desire to develop a startup because I was there too.

But I knew I could do more, I felt that it was n't right for me to work at a job from 9:00 to 17:00 and I wanted to be remembered as someone who did a little more than getting through life and because I opened my first startup called: Payvolt.io which was successfully sold!

I went through ups and downs, abnormal difficulties, full of mistakes, sleepless nights, wasted money, layoffs/hiring and more... But I didn't stop there, being an entrepreneur I immediately looked for new ventures to work on and that's how I ended up at Matirilize.
