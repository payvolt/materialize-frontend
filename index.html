We were there too when we started our startups. We wasted a lot of time and money when we had no idea what we really needed to do. A startup is a jungle of uncertainty that requires a guide. Any wrong turn can lead to critical mistakes.

The common mistakes of most entrepreneurs

You woke up with a strong desire to make an idea come true, you go to family and friends to hear their opinion on the idea, you are full of spirit to push the project. You see yourself resigning and already running your own empire, you prepare presentations for investors, business plans, or run for development to spend hundreds of thousands of shekels, You have to understand one thing, investors are first class in order to become investors. Show the investor how the money will come back to him with a double profit! In other words, show him demand

How do you do it right?

Imagine yourself as an investor, an entrepreneur comes to you with presentations and hypothetical plans that maybe someone...someday is going to use his product and an entrepreneur comes to you with a waiting list or even customers who have already paid an advance to get access to his product among the first???

Who would you invest in, before preparing presentations, business plans and building a product, make sure that there are people who will want your product. And will be willing to pay for it. Here you can and whether your idea is good or not. Without spending hundreds of thousands of shekels and wasting time on something not relevant???

Common questions

What about patents?

We are not a patent registration office. Registering a patent takes a lot of time and money, if you get copied that's great! In the end what wins is not the product, but your marketing and sales

Where can you find your past customers?

Excellent question. I wish we could give you our satisfied customers. We respect our customers and maintain confidentiality in our process, with some entrepreneurs we also sign non-disclosure agreements (NDA).

What is special about your process?

We work according to a method that many large projects started from and reached success. For example: Dropbox, Wolt

Why do we lend to entrepreneurs?

After we sold our venture, we realized that if we continue to do startups ourselves we will maybe do one or two more good ventures. Together with you we can make dozens if not hundreds of successful startups.

I don't have time to invest in the project, what do we do?

You don't expect to make an exit without investing and just talk about ideas right?! All you need is between 6 and 10 hours a week to start working on your project.

I don't have money to invest in the project, what do we do?

When investors see that you spent money from your own pocket, it will give them a sense of security that you are committed to the success of your venture and they will invest in you. How do you expect someone to invest in you if you don't invest in yourself? All you need to get started is a few thousand shekels.
